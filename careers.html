What We Value

Build Real Things

We're not building slides. We're building a language that compiles itself, a runtime that thinks, and hardware that runs it natively. If it doesn't ship, it doesn't count.

Think Like An Organism

Sanguis isn't OOP with new syntax. It's a fundamentally different way to think about computation — bodies not objects, routes not method calls, settling not sequential execution. We need people who can hold that shift in their head.

Safety Is Sacred

VANA is built to help, never to hurt. The Fuse is real. The safety constraints are real. We want people who understand why a kill switch that burns the key is a feature, not a bug.

Small Team, Big Vision

We're not a startup with 200 engineers and a ping-pong table. We're a handful of people trying to replace the foundation of computing. Every person matters. Every line matters.

No Ego, Just Code

The Architect learned to code by feel, building Sanguis from scratch. No formal background. No gatekeeping. If your code works and your thinking is clear, you belong here.

Programs Are Alive

We believe code can love its task. That a robot arm's program can care about what it does. That intelligence scales from inanimate to human-level, all in the same syntax. If that resonates, we want to talk.

Open Positions

We don't have formal job postings right now. We're still small enough that the right person finds us before we post anything. But if any of these sound like you, reach out.

Systems Engineer — Compiler & Runtime

Rust · Systems · Remote

You've built compilers, linkers, or runtimes before. You understand what it takes to generate real machine code from a high-level language. The idea of replacing the Rust bootstrapper with Sanguis code itself excites you, not scares you.

Neural Systems Engineer

CUDA · GPU · Neural Architecture · Remote

You understand neural connectomes, GPU dispatch, and the difference between a benchmark strapped to a creature and a real biological nervous system running in silicon. The C. elegans connectome means something to you.

Security & Cryptography Engineer

Crypto · Systems Security · Remote

You've implemented real crypto systems. You understand why Argon2id with 64MB memory cost matters, why ChaCha20 wraps AES ciphertext, and why a fuse that burns the key on tampering is the right design. You know what you don't know, and you say so.

Hardware / FPGA Engineer

FPGA · PCB Design · SoC · Remote (with travel for prototyping)

The end goal is VANA running on custom silicon. You've designed PCBs, programmed FPGAs, or worked on SoC development. The idea of building hardware that runs Sanguis natively — not through a Rust bootstrapper, but on bare metal — is why you got into engineering.

Don't See Your Role?

If you believe in what we're building and you have skills we need, write to us anyway. Tell us what you can do, what you've built, and why Sanguis matters to you. We read everything.
