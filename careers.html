Careers

We're building a programming language where programs are alive. If that's the kind of problem you want to work on, we want to talk.

VolkanaDEV is pre-funding. Roles are equity-only until we raise. When funding comes through, everyone converts to salaried W-2 with full benefits. No managers, no fixed schedule, no clock. You do the work, we handle the rest.

How We Work

Ship, don't present

Working code beats slides. Every time.

Safety is non-negotiable

VANA is built to help, never to hurt. The fuse is real. If you don't understand why that matters, this isn't the right fit.

No managers

Flat structure. You choose what to work on. The Architect sets direction, not your schedule. If you need someone to tell you what to do, this won't work.

Depth over headcount

We hire slow and keep the team small. Every person changes the product.

Show your work

We care about what you can build, not where you learned to build it. Put your work at the top of your application.

Remote, async, flexible

No office. No standups. No time tracking. Work when you're sharp. We measure output.

Open Roles

Systems Engineer - Compiler & Runtime

Equity-only · Remote · C / Systems

Work on the Sanguis compiler and VANA runtime. The long-term goal is replacing the C runtime scaffold with self-hosted Sanguis code.

  • Experience building compilers, linkers, or language runtimes
  • Strong C and systems programming background
  • Comfortable working without a roadmap - you'll help write it
Apply

Neural Systems Engineer

Equity-only · Remote · CUDA / GPU / Neural

Scale the VANA Cortex to GPU hardware. You'll work on GPU dispatch for large-scale neural simulations and help build out the cortex architecture.

  • CUDA or GPU compute dispatch experience
  • Understanding of neural architecture and connectomics
  • Ability to work with simulations at million-node scale
Apply

Security & Cryptography Engineer

Equity-only · Remote · Crypto / Systems Security

Work on the security layer. Maintain, test, and extend the integrity verification system. You'll help protect VANA from tampering and unauthorized modification.

  • Production crypto implementation experience
  • Deep understanding of authenticated encryption and KDFs
  • Side-channel attack mitigation knowledge
Apply

Hardware / FPGA Engineer

Equity-only · Remote (travel for prototyping) · FPGA / PCB / SoC

The end goal is VANA on custom silicon. Design and prototype the hardware pathway - FPGA boards, custom PCBs, eventually SoC.

  • Custom PCB design from schematic to fabrication
  • FPGA development (Verilog, VHDL, or HLS)
  • Hardware/software co-design experience
Apply

Deployment Consultant

  • Comfortable working independently and managing client relationships
