Systems Engineer — Compiler & Runtime

Full-time · Remote · Rust / Systems

Work on the Sanguis compiler and VANA runtime. The long-term goal is replacing the Rust bootstrapper with self-hosted Sanguis code.

  • Experience building compilers, linkers, or language runtimes
  • Strong Rust and systems programming background
  • Comfortable working without a roadmap — you'll help write it
Apply

Neural Systems Engineer

Full-time · Remote · CUDA / GPU / Neural

Scale the VANA Cortex to GPU hardware. The brain is based on the real C. elegans connectome — not a benchmark strapped to a creature.

  • CUDA or GPU compute dispatch experience
  • Understanding of neural architecture and connectomics
  • Ability to work with simulations at million-node scale
Apply

Security & Cryptography Engineer

Full-time · Remote · Crypto / Systems Security

Own the Fuse — five stacked crypto layers (SHA-256, HMAC-SHA3-256, AES-256-GCM, ChaCha20-Poly1305, Argon2id). Maintain, test, and extend it.

  • Production crypto implementation experience
  • Deep understanding of authenticated encryption and KDFs
  • Side-channel attack mitigation knowledge
Apply

Hardware / FPGA Engineer

Full-time · Remote (travel for prototyping) · FPGA / PCB / SoC

The end goal is VANA on custom silicon. Design and prototype the hardware pathway — FPGA boards, custom PCBs, eventually SoC.

  • Custom PCB design from schematic to fabrication
  • FPGA development (Verilog, VHDL, or HLS)
  • Hardware/software co-design experience

How we hire

1
You write

Email us. Tell us what you've built and why you want to work on this. Link to your work or attach a resume.

2
We talk

30-minute call. We discuss what you've built and what we're building. No whiteboard, no leetcode.

3
Technical conversation

Deeper discussion about your area. We might walk through a real problem we're facing. It's collaborative.

4
Offer

If it's a fit, we move fast. We respect your time.

Don't see your role?
