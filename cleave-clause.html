Cleave Clause Master Agreement

The private government infrastructure contract for VolkanaDEV defense deployments.

This document supplements the public VPL-1.0 license and is negotiated on a per-project basis. To request a copy, contact volkanadev@volkanadev.com.

Absolute Custody

The Licensee receives full physical and operational custody. Zero Licensor access, zero telemetry, zero backdoors. The boundary is absolute.

Signed Updates, No Interference

Cryptographically signed security patches delivered on a staging timeline. No operational interference with Licensee systems.

Defense-Only Override

Overrides VPL-1.0 Section 2 for approved defense deployments. Unauthorized use terminates the contract instantly.

The FrameworkHow the Cleave Clause Works

The Cleave Clause is a private contract for government agencies and defense entities that need to deploy VolkanaDEV technology for defense purposes. It overrides the ethical use restrictions in Section 2 of the VPL-1.0 on a per-project basis, granting a private license for a specific approved scope of use.

Each project receives its own Exhibit A, defining operational boundaries, deployment parameters, and project-specific compliance requirements. One agency may use Exhibit A for automated border missile intercept validation, while another may use it for submarine hull stress simulation. Exhibit A is negotiated and signed separately for each project.

Public distribution of the Software does not pass through this contract. All public distribution channels are maintained exclusively by VolkanaDEV Autonomous Systems, Inc. The Licensee's rights under this Master Agreement are limited to the Approved Scope defined in Exhibit A. Unauthorized distribution, deployment, or use outside the Approved Scope constitutes an incurable material breach.

To request a copy of the full Master Agreement, contact volkanadev@volkanadev.com. This page presents the key provisions. The full contract is negotiated and signed separately for each project.
