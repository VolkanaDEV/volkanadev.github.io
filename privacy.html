1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us through the contact form on this website. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and organizational affiliation (if provided).
  • Inquiry Details: The category of inquiry you select (consumer, corporate, or government), the contents of your message, and any deployment details you choose to share.
  • Technical Information: Basic information transmitted by your email client when you submit the contact form, such as your email headers and IP address. We do not collect this independently; it is included by your email provider in standard email transmission.

We do not collect browsing data, page view analytics, device fingerprints, or behavioral profiles. We do not require you to create an account.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information you submit for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry: Reviewing your submission and replying with relevant information, contract details, or follow-up questions.
  • To evaluate procurement requests: Assessing government carve-out contract requests, corporate deployment proposals, or consumer access requests.
  • To maintain records of correspondence: Keeping a record of communications for legal, contractual, and operational purposes.
  • To protect our rights: Retaining correspondence that may be relevant to enforcing our license terms, intellectual property rights, or contractual obligations.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not add you to a mailing list unless you explicitly request it.

3. HOW WE STORE YOUR INFORMATION

When you submit the contact form, your email client generates a message addressed to volkanadev@volkanadev.com. This message is transmitted via standard email protocols and stored in our email inbox. The specifics:

  • Where it lives: Your submission is stored as an email message in our Google Workspace inbox hosted on Google's infrastructure. It is not stored in a separate database, customer relationship management system, or cloud form service.
  • Who can see it: Only authorized personnel at VolkanaDEV with access to the volkanadev@volkanadev.com inbox can read your submission. No third parties have access.
  • How long it stays: See Section 6 (Data Retention) below.

4. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We do not use cookies on this website. We do not use web beacons, tracking pixels, session identifiers, or local storage for analytics or advertising purposes. We do not embed social media plugins that track visitors. If you navigate away from this website and visit a third-party link, that third party's privacy policy applies.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit this website, GitHub may log standard server-level request data (IP address, request time, user agent) as part of its normal hosting infrastructure. This data is governed by GitHub's privacy policy, not ours. We do not control or have access to GitHub's server logs.

When you submit the contact form, your email is routed through your email provider and Google Workspace (our email host). Both providers handle transmission and storage according to their own privacy policies and security standards. We do not add any additional tracking to this process.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain correspondence submitted through the contact form for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of the inquiry. Specifically:

  • General inquiries: Retained for up to 12 months after the inquiry is resolved, then deleted.
  • Corporate and procurement requests: Retained for the duration of any active evaluation or contract negotiation, plus 12 months after completion.
  • Government contract requests: Retained for the duration of the Master Government Infrastructure Contract and any applicable regulatory retention requirements, which may extend beyond the contract term.
  • Legal holds: If correspondence is relevant to an active legal matter, we retain it until the matter is resolved, regardless of the timelines above.

You may request early deletion of your correspondence at any time by emailing us (see Section 13). We will comply unless a legal or contractual obligation requires retention.

7. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you submit. These measures include:

  • Restricting access to the volkanadev@volkanadev.com inbox to authorized personnel only.
  • Using Google Workspace's built-in security features, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Not storing form submissions in external databases or third-party form services.
  • Not transmitting sensitive information (passwords, financial data, classified material) through the contact form. If you need to share sensitive information, we will provide a secure channel after initial contact.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your information using the tools available to us.

8. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the correspondence you have submitted to us.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information you have provided.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your correspondence, subject to legal and contractual retention obligations.
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your information for specific purposes.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent for any future communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email volkanadev@volkanadev.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

9. INTERNATIONAL USERS

This website is operated from the United States. If you access this website from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using this website and submitting information to us, you consent to this transfer. We comply with applicable data protection laws to the extent they apply to our practices.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us through the contact form, email us and we will promptly delete it.

11. DO NOT TRACK SIGNALS

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you do not want your browsing activity tracked. Because we do not use cookies, analytics, or behavioral tracking on this website, Do Not Track signals do not change how this website operates. We do not track you regardless of whether this signal is present.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. If we make material changes that affect how we handle previously collected information, we will provide a prominent notice on the main website. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Because we do not collect enough information to notify you individually of changes, we will not send direct notifications. The updated policy takes effect for all submissions made after the posted date.

13. CONTACT US

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your personal information, contact us at:

  • Email: volkanadev@volkanadev.com
  • Entity: VolkanaDEV Autonomous Systems, Inc.
  • Subject: Privacy Policy Inquiry

We are committed to working with you to resolve any concerns you may have about how your information is handled.
