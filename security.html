Safety & Licenses

This technology is powerful. We built absolute rules to make sure it is used to help, never to hurt. These rules are not optional. They are enforced by license, by contract, and by code.

The Shared Code Promise

You can read, test, and adapt our code freely. If you modify the engine and run it for users over a network, you must release your changes publicly. No secret monopolies.

Strict Anti-Harm Rules

Our license forbids this framework from being used for autonomous weapons, mass surveillance, facial recognition without consent, or tools designed to exploit people.

Immediate Toxic Breach

Break the anti-harm rules and your license terminates instantly. You assume all legal liability, lose all security updates, and are permanently locked out of VolkanaDEV networks.

The FrameworkHow the Rules Work

Computational power belongs in the hands of the individual creator. But technology this advanced, left unregulated, creates an environment where corporations or actors can abuse it.

That is why we created the VPL-1.0. It keeps our software open and inspectable for independent developers, students, and researchers, while installing a legal wall against weaponization or exploitation.

For government agencies and large entities that need to deploy outside the public ethical restrictions, we offer a separate contract: the Master Government Infrastructure Contract. It grants a private, perpetual, non-transferable, non-sublicensable license for a specific approved scope. It overrides Section 2 of the VPL-1.0 on a per-project basis. It requires formal contract execution. See the Contact page to request one.

The Master Contract includes four binding provisions: a carve-out that supersedes the public ethical restrictions in the context of autonomous defense systems, a system handoff that gives the Licensee absolute custody with zero Licensor data access, a maintenance clause requiring cryptographically signed security updates with no operational interference, and an indemnification clause delivering the engine "as is" with full Licensee liability. These are summarized in Section 5 of the license below.
